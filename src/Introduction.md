This is a book about Kubernetes Concepts. Unlikely the traditional books, which explains things from Group up, this explains based on the problem statements.

The book is aimed towards peopel who already know kubernetes through the traditional books. If you haven't ready any of those and do not know Kubernetes,  then this book can be confusing to you.
However,  please do not bother, take as much as you can, and if you want enjoy the comics at the end of each chapter.

I wrote this based on the problems I have come across and things that interest me. This will be a living book, and I will refine it with examples.  I wrote it for myself. I wanted to understand somethings, and what better way than to pursue it by asking questions and finding answers.  This will certainly be useful to other engineers and architects who have pragmatic outlook for engineering problems. Who want to understand things and want to dwell deep. 

All these chapters make a very interesting discussion points.
